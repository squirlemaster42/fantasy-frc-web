## ü§î What is Fantasy FRC?

Fantasy FRC is a fantasy league game for FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC), similar to fantasy football but with FRC teams instead of NFL players. You'll:

- **Draft FRC teams** during a live draft with other players
- **Earn points** based on how your teams perform in real FRC competitions
- **Compete against friends** to see who can build the best fantasy team portfolio
- **Follow along** during championship events to track your teams' success

### The Concept
Just like in traditional fantasy sports, you become a "team owner" who selects a portfolio of real FRC teams. Your fantasy score is the sum of all your drafted teams' actual competition performance scores.

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need to know about FRC to play?**
Not at all! The game is designed to be accessible to everyone, though FRC fans might have an advantage in team recognition.

**How long does a season last?**
Most scoring happens during championship weekend, but drafts can be set up anytime. A typical "season" spans the championship event period.

**What if I miss my draft pick?**
The system has auto-skip functionality to keep drafts moving, but it's best to be present for your picks!

**Can I trade teams after the draft?**
Currently, teams are locked after the draft. The challenge is in your initial draft strategy!
